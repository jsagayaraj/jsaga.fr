Hôtel du Midi - Welcome

A warm welcome awaits you at the Hotel du Midi*** in Montpellier, situated on the famous Place de la Comédie, opposite the Opera house and the iconic Three Graces fountain. Its ideal location within walking distance of the train station and the tram enables you to head out and explore the historic city center, the Corum and the ultra-modern Antigone district.

Découvrir Montpellier

Montpellier, the capital of the Languedoc-Roussillon region of southern France, is 10km inland from the Mediterranean Sea. Its medieval center, the Ecusson, is home to narrow alleyways and the pedestrian-only Rue de la Loge, where shops sell antiques, crafts and upmarket fashion www.montpellier.fr
